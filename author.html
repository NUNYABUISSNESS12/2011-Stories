About the Author

Hey there! I'm Bentley Playz, a 13-year-old writer who’s passionate about creating exciting stories that take you on unforgettable adventures. Despite being young, I’ve been writing and creating for a long time, and I'm just getting started!

I’m super proud of everything I’ve written, and it’s my dream to one day publish these stories as real books. I hope you enjoy reading them as much as I love writing them. I’m always working on new ideas, and who knows—maybe you’ll see my name on the cover of a book in the future!

At 13, I’ve got tons of creativity and big plans. You’ll be hearing more from me, so keep an eye out. One day, you’ll be able to say you read my stories before they hit the shelves!

Thanks for being part of this journey with me. Who knows what cool adventures are ahead!
