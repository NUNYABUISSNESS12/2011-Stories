Chapter 17: The Space Quest

I was going to wish for a giant pool of cheese. Suddenly, Zack called. He said, “There was a shortage of magic.” I replied, “What do you mean a shortage of magic?” He said, “Well, my friend, try using the football.”

I put down the phone real quick and said, “I wish for $1,000,000,000.” Nothing happened. I picked up the phone again. I said, “How do we get it back?” Zack replied, “We’ll have to wait. There’s no guarantee.” I replied again, “WHAT DO YOU MEAN?” Zack said on the phone, “Someone is using a magic block somewhere in space.”

I said, “SPACE?” Zack replied, “Yes, I think so.” I told him, “We need to meet IRL (in real life).” Zack hangs up and uses his teleport wand. “How did you get here with magic? I thought we were out?” Zack said, “My wand has only a little bit of magic left.”

I told Zack, “We need a spaceship and gear. Can you do it?” I saw a sparkly wand that Zack held and said, “Upon us the enchanted gear” into the mic. At that second, the doorbell rang. It was a NASA agent with the gear. He said, “Buddy, I'm from NASA. Don’t break the equipment or you won’t get your deposit back.”

As we went to space, Zack coached me on the screen on what to do: “Okay, click Start, then set the cords to S,U,P Satellite.” I headed there. I saw a glowing satellite with a device called "blocker" and the signal was damaged. I repaired it, and all magic was restored. Zack confirmed it.
